CLIENTS are encouraged to enjoy gaming in a responsible manner. However, we do recognize that gaming can become addictive and problematic for some people. To help CLIENTS avoid potential problems we offer short breaks. If a CLIENT finds that they are unable to control their play, we recommend that they consider a break or a self-exclusion. Self-exclusion can vary in length from six months to indefinite.

 Self-exclusion enables a CLIENT to request in writing, that we block them from using our services. Thereafter, our representatives will get in touch with you to confirm your request after which an email confirming the exclusion will be sent to you.  Kindly note that our representatives will respond to your request within 48 hours of initial receipt. However, should this not occur it is your responsibility to send out a follow up email to our support team.

 It is the responsibility of the CLIENT to adhere to any self-exclusion agreement, though we are happy to help by blacklisting addresses and devices where possible (please note, this is not always possible due to technical restrictions).

The effectiveness of any self-exclusion agreement depends largely on the commitment of the CLIENT to manage their own behavior. An exclusion will be most effective if a CLIENT will agree to not attempt to register again. Please be advised that exclusion should not be intended to function as a replacement for willpower or as a full guarantee to stop the CLIENT from further gaming.

Once the self-exclusion period is activated, our support team will reach out to you for instructions on whether you wish to withdraw or retain the balance of money in your account. If any bets will be pending and in the event you happen to win on the bets, our team will also contact you to make arrangements for the payment of your winnings.
