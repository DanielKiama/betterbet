  • How do you withdraw?

    SMS WITHDRAW#amount TO 29444. Example WITHDRAW#50 to 29444Or visit www.flooks.com/profile/withdraw

    Why can’t I withdraw my entire credit balance?

    Make sure that your current credit balance qualifies for the minimum withdrawal amount (Ksh 50) together with the applicable fees as indicated below:

    Between

    • KES. 50 – 1,000: Applicable fee is KES. 15
    • KES. 1,001 – 1,500: Applicable fee is KES. 25
    • KES. 1,501 – 70,000: Applicable fee is KES.33

    In addition, all specified amounts must be whole numbers with no decimal points or commas. If you are participating in a bonus promotion with your Flooks account, you may not request withdrawal until all the requirements for participation have been satisfied.

    I am unable to withdraw my winnings.

    Make sure that your current credit balance qualifies for the minimum withdrawal specified in your account under Withdrawals. Also make sure that you did not use special characters when filling in the withdrawal form. Decimal points, commas, colons, etc. should not be used.

    I have requested a withdrawal, but the amount has not been transferred to my mobile money wallet yet.

    All withdrawals take approximately up to 5 to 60 seconds. Call the customer care team on 0704/0705 999444 for further assistance.

  • How do I place a bet via web?

    To place a bet, you first need to log in to www.flooks.com and then perform the following steps:

    • Browse the sports betting section (access sport and leagues via navigation bar and available filters)
    • Tap the odds value of the market you wish to add to your betslip
    • Access the betslip from the header by tapping on in the top right-hand corner or via the yellow confirmation message displayed under the selected odds
    • Select your bet type: Single, Multi or System
    • Enter the stake you wish to wager on your selected bets
    • Tap the “Place bet” button (if your selections are correct) to submit your betslip
    • The confirmation page will be displayed upon successful processing
    Where do I find live games for live betting?

    Browse the live sports betting section (access the “Live” filter via the navigation bar or via the sports homepage)

    Why would my Bets not be accepted on my betslip?

    The “Place bet” button may switch its message under certain conditions. For example, when odds value(s) for a selected bet change (e.g. odds value increase), the “Place bet” will switch to “Accept new odds”. Once you press this button, the bet slip will accept all odd changes with one tap. The new odd values will be accepted and your “Possible winnings” will be recalculated based on the new odds values. Finally, after tapping on the “Accept new odds” button, the system will return to the “Place bet” button in order to continue with the submission of the bet. Alternatively ensure you have sufficient balance in your account or if you are placing a bonus bet ensure you satisfy all bonus bet conditions on odds.

  • I bet on a game that was postponed. What will happen to my bet?

    If a betting event fails to take place as scheduled, for any reason, or if it ends without an official score and is not resumed within 72 hours, the stakes will be refunded or credited to your account. If you picked the game for a Multi (parlay) or system bet, the pick will be evaluated at odds of 1 to 1.

    My bet has not been settled yet. Why not?

    First of all, please check the detailed view of the corresponding bet under “My account”/“My bets” to make sure that the betting event has already taken place. If the event has already taken place and more than 30 minutes have elapsed since the announcement of the results but your bet is still open, please contact our customer support team, indicating the corresponding bet ID (as displayed in the detailed view of the bet). Please note that there may sometimes be delays in the evaluation because the results must be confirmed by official sources. In the case of live bets, the bets are evaluated in real time as the game progresses. In individual cases, there may be delays in the settlement. For example, it may be necessary to review the film footage in order to ensure a correct evaluation. This cannot be done until the game is over, since managing the live bet generally takes priority.

    My bet was settled incorrectly.

    If you believe that we have made a mistake in the settlement of your bet, please contact our customer support team on 0704/0705 999444, indicating the corresponding bet ID. If you can substantiate your claim based on an official source (e.g. a link to the results on the official club site), please include this additional information in your query.

    My player/driver/team failed to show up at the event. What happens to my bet?

    The procedure depends on the type of sport and the kind of bet you placed. For the full details, see our Sports betting - General remarks and Special Rules - Overview.

    Why was my bet cancelled?

    Bets may be cancelled by Flooks for a variety of reasons. Please refer to our Sports betting - General remarks. Cancelled bets will only be displayed in grey in your online betting account under “My bets”, and the result is displayed as “CANCELLED”.
